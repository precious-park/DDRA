Our Mission

The Disease Detection and Response Alliance (DDRA) is dedicated to improving healthcare through innovation, research, and technology. We focus on enhancing disease detection, monitoring, and response systems, particularly in underserved regions.

Infectious Disease

Monitoring, detection, and response to infectious disease outbreaks with advanced technology.

Antimicrobial Resistance

Developing solutions to address drug-resistant pathogens through global collaboration.

Vaccines Research

Supporting development and distribution of vaccines to protect vulnerable populations.

ROP Screening System

Our specialized system for Retinopathy of Prematurity (ROP) screening is designed to protect the vision of premature infants. ROP is a potentially blinding eye disorder that primarily affects premature babies, and early detection is crucial for effective treatment.

  • Track premature infants at risk for ROP
  • Manage screening schedules effectively
  • Record diagnoses and treatment plans
  • Enable multi-hospital coordination
  • Generate reports for healthcare planning
